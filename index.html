Programs

Your connection. Your way.

Our "General Penpairing" Program

Connecting penpals together for social purposes.

1.For patients: you have the choice to match with either fellow patient penpals and/or all penpals.
2.For non-patients: you may only match with patient penpals. (After all, that's why you're here right??)
3.For everyone: You may only form two penpairs at the same time as we highly value the quality of our penpair relationships. (FAQ for more!)

Our "Research Penpairing" Program

Connecting penpals and medical professionals together for research purposes.

1.For patient penpals: If you are open to it - you also have the choice to connect for research purposes.
2.For researchers: You MUST indicate that you are a research penpal (and not a general non-patient penpal).
3.For everyone: There are no limits to the number of research based penpairs.

Our "Sponsor-a-Patient" Program

Donate to help save a life!

1. For patients: If you seek donations, you may fill out an application in order for us to broadcast your profile.
2. For donors: After viewing verified profiles, you may only donate the verified needed amount.
3. For everyone: At Penpairs, 100% of Sponsor-A-Patient donation go directly to the patient you want to sponsor.

Common Problems

  • Patients have limited to no interaction with persons outside of the hospital
  • In person volunteers are entirely domestic persons, and are frequently disallowed due to health precautions
  • Persons seek to connect to terminally ill patients for both therapeutic, and humanitarian reasons outside of select hospitals
  • Many penpaling sites loosely encourage/disclose private contact information between users for letter sending
  • Donors donate to certain health causes with little autonomy in choosing the exact destination of their donation

Our Solutions

  • App/site connects patients of all ages with virtual penpals from all over the world
  • Penpals are matched via the level of similarities based on their application or by swiping right in the pool of persons
  • Penpals can choose to optionally send both virtual and/or non-virtual gifts via our site/app
  • Both penpals’ and patients’ contact information remain private and one can only interact through our medium
  • For secure visits - penpals pay a premium to send visiting permission applications to either hospitals or to patient’s family for supervised outpatient visits
  • Penpals view verified stories of financially disadvantaged patients and can only donate the verified needed amount
